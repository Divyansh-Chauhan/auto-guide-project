What is Customer Segmentation?


Customer segmentation is the process of analysing and understanding the customer in coherence to your industry. So as to catalogue those customers into subgroups according to their attributes (age, sex etc.) and preferences.


Why it's important for the automotive industry to segment customers?


In B2C industries (such as the automotive industry), having the capability of putting your customers into subgroups helps in 2 ways:

1. Helps in decreasing customer acquisition costs by creating niche groups inside a big dataset, so as to understand what kind of product will be liked by which kind of people. If companies know what will their audience like and not like, then they can do better-targeted marketing.

CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) has been increasing in the past few years and becoming a “real threat” as quoted by Maruti Chairman R.C. Bhargava. Thus if a company decides to build a car and can have cheaper methods of acquiring customers then they can price themselves better than the competition.

2. Helps in designing the product and understand the prospective market by letting the manufacturer know the current demands and pain points of the customers. Also helps in forming clusters of consumers that might be interested in specific combinations of products just by understanding their previous purchase decisions.
