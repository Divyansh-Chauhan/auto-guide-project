What is Location Analysis?


Location analysis is understanding the attributes and distinctiveness of the region you're trying to sell in on the basis of infrastructure and population that resides in that location


Why it's important for the automotive industry to do location analysis?


For the automotive industry, having an indepth knowledge of the location you're trying to sell in, helps in 2 ways:

1. Gives the industry a better sense of what kind of product will be apt for what region as a product might be fitting in terms of the demographics and behvaiours of the population but might not be operable in that certain location. For example: a car with very high engine power wouldn't sell where roads are not good enough to take benefit out of it.

2. Helps in deciding the regions where manufacturing units could be setup as the process of manufacturing is very intense and requires a lot of natural resources and tranportable resources. Therefore a place with even a more expensive intial setup cost might be worth it in the long term by reducing running Cost associated to it.
