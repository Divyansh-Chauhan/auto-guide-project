What is the perfect launch time for automobiles?


Once a product is made and it is ready to be sold, one needs to plan the timeline as to when it's supposed to be launched on the basis of verious parameters: Occasions, Festivals, Consumer sentiment, brand image at that time etc.


Why it's important to launch a product at a specific time?


Launch time can make or break a product. if you launch your product in a time where people don't buy that product. for example: launching an AC in winters. What happens is that the AC loses it's launch hype.

Sentiment Prediction


The best place to learn the sentiments of the market is through Twitter, there you can see what everyone is saying. What all can you achieve from twitter sentiment analysis:

1. What people are saying about your competiton.
2. What is the market sentiment towards the product that you're trying to launch.
3. What are people saying about your products.

Now to achieve all this, just type in the name of the company or person for whom you want to check the market sentiment.
After that type in the number of tweets that you want to assess the results upon. The tweet collection will start from the moment you press enter.
